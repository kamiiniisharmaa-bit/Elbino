Rooted in purpose 🌱

We believe commerce can heal the planet.

Elbino was born from a simple idea: everyday products shouldn't cost the Earth. Since 2020, we've partnered with ethical makers, farmers, and innovators to bring you goods that are 100% plastic-free, carbon-conscious, and beautiful.

From bamboo utensils to upcycled fashion, every item supports regenerative practices and fair wages. We’re not just a shop — we’re a movement.

Our core beliefs

Planet First

Carbon-neutral shipping, recycled packaging, and 1% of revenue donated to reforestation.

Ethical Production

Every partner meets fair labor standards. We prioritize local artisans & fair wages.

Circular Design

From compostable wraps to zero-waste kits, we design out waste before it's created.

How it started

In 2020, our founders met at a climate strike — and realized that shopping sustainably was too complicated and expensive. We launched Elbino to bridge the gap between style, affordability, and eco-integrity. What began as a small online market has grown into a community of over 50k conscious consumers.

Today we're proud to be a B Corp candidate, working towards full transparency. Every product on our site meets strict environmental and social criteria.
