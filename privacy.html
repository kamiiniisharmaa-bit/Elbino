Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority — we protect your data like we protect the planet.

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

At EcoMerch, we are committed to respecting your privacy and protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, make a purchase, or interact with our services. We follow strict data minimization principles — just like we minimize waste.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a seamless and secure shopping experience:

  • Personal identifiers: Name, email address, shipping address, phone number (for order updates).
  • Payment details: Processed securely via PCI-compliant gateways; we never store full credit card numbers.
  • Browsing data: Cookies and usage analytics to improve site performance and recommend eco-friendly products.
  • Account preferences: Wishlist, past orders, and sustainability preferences.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to fulfill orders, send shipping confirmations, personalize your shopping experience, and share occasional updates about sustainable living (only if you opt in). We never sell your personal information to third parties. Ever.

  • ✅ Process and deliver your eco-friendly products
  • ✅ Provide customer support and returns
  • ✅ Improve our website and product recommendations
  • ✅ Send you green tips and exclusive offers (with your consent)

Sharing & Disclosure

We only share your data with essential service providers:

  • Shipping carriers (e.g., USPS, DHL) to deliver your order.
  • Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) for secure transactions.
  • Legal compliance when required by law or to protect our rights.

All partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and cannot use your data for any other purpose.

Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal, privacy-friendly cookies to remember your cart items and preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work optimally. We do not use invasive tracking or sell analytics data.

Your Rights

You have full control over your personal data. At any time, you can:

  • 🔍 Request access to the data we hold about you.
  • ✏️ Correct or update inaccurate information.
  • 🗑️ Request deletion of your account and personal data.
  • 📧 Opt out of marketing emails via the "unsubscribe" link.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@ecomerch.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

EcoMerch does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover such data, we delete it immediately. Our products are intended for adult shoppers.

International Transfers

We are based in the US, but we ship worldwide. If you are in the EU or UK, your data is protected under GDPR standards. We ensure adequate safeguards for international transfers.
